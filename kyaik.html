Zinkyaik Waterfall

English Myanmar

English

Zin Kyaik Waterfall, located near Zin Kyaik village in Thaton township of Mon State, Myanmar. The depth of Zin Kyaik waterfall is not specified in available information. However, it is described as one of the highest waterfalls in Myanmar. The waterfall is especially popular during the rainy season, from May to October, when the water flow is abundant, creating a refreshing pool suitable for swimming.
