Ngwe-Saung Beach

English Myanmar

English

Ngwe Saung Beach, also known as Silver Beach, is a popular beach resort located on the western coast of Myanmar, It's situated about 48 km west of Patheinin TownShip in the Ayeyarwady Region and 188 km from Yangon. A popular resort with tourists from October to April.Ngwe Saung Beach is a stunning coastal destination located on the Bay of Bengal, in the Ayeyarwady Region of Myanmar. Just a five-hour drive from Yangon, this peaceful beach town offers visitors a quiet escape from the city's chaos. Ngwe Saung, which means “Silver Beach,” lives up to its name with its long stretches of soft, silvery-white sand and crystal-clear waters.Fringed with palm trees and charming resorts, it's the perfect spot for sunbathing, swimming, and fresh seafood dining. The nearby islands and snorkeling opportunities attract nature lovers, while the romantic sunsets make it ideal for couples. Less crowded than other Southeast Asian beaches, Ngwe Saung is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Travelers seeking relaxation, beauty, and local charm should not miss this coastal paradise.
