Kyon Htwe Waterfall

English Myanmar

English

Kyone Htaw Waterfall is located in Hlaing Bwe Township, Karen State. The waterfall is located north of Hpa-an City, about a 90-minute to 2-hour drive away. Kyone Htaw Waterfall is approximately 50 miles (80 kilometers) from Shanywarthit, which is part of Hlaingbwe Township. Best Time to Visit Kyone Htaw Waterfall in HpaAn, Myanmar is during the dry season, which runs from November to February.
