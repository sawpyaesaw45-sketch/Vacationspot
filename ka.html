Kanthaya Beach

English Myanmar

English

Kanthaya Beach in Myanmar is on the Bay of Bengal in Rakhine State. It is about 100 km (60 miles) south of the better-known Ngapali Beach. The official name in English “Pleasant Beach” is a direct translation of the Myanmar name. And it is the most suitable one as the place is indeed very pleasant with all the natural beauties of unspoiled nature. The best time to visit Kan Thar Yar Beach, and Myanmar in general, is during the cool, dry season, which runs from November to February.
