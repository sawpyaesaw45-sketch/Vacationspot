Set Sae Beach

English Myanmar

English

SET SAE Beach, nestled in the tranquil Than Byu Zayat Township of Mawlamyine, is a captivating coastal attraction that beckons tourists with its scenic beauty and inviting atmosphere. Spanning a vast stretch of golden sand, this beach offers visitors the perfect setting to unwind, enjoy the sun, and revel in the refreshing ocean breeze. The beach is particularly popular for its gentle to moderate waves, making it an ideal spot for swimming and floating on tubes, as many locals and visitors have delightfully noted. The aesthetic charm of SET SAE Beach is enhanced by its backdrop of lush greenery, creating a serene environment that is perfect for relaxation and picturesque photography. The best time to visit Setse Sea Beach in Myanmar is generally during the dry season, which typically runs from November to April.
